Assam Flood Kill 4, Over 2,000 Villages Still Under Water
About 1.45 lakh hectares of agricultural land is under flood waters. 
Guwahati: At least four people have died in Assam over the last 24 hours, as the state still grapples with a flood that has barely shown signs of retreat over the past few days.
