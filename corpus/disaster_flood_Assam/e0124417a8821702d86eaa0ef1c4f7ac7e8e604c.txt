RAINFALL/FLOOD SITUATION IN THE COUNTRY
 The southwest monsoon has been vigorous over West Madhya Pradesh and Vidarbha and active over Marathwada and Andaman & Nicobar Islands. Indian Meteorological Department (Major features of weather forecast)
Rain/thundershowers has occurred at most places over Gangetic West Bengal, Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Vidarbha, Coastal Karnataka and Andaman & Nicobar Islands; at many places over Assam & Meghalaya, East Rajasthan, Odisha, Gujarat region, Madhya Maharashtra, Konkan & Goa and Kerala; at a few places over Himachal Pradesh, Bihar, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram & Tripura, Arunachal Pradesh, Interior Karnataka and Telangana at isolated places over Uttarakhand, West Uttar Pradesh, Tamilnadu and Lakshadweep. Weather remained mainly dry/dry over rest parts of the country. The amounts of rainfall (4 cm or more) recorded at 0830 hours IST of today are: Khandwa 37; Dhar & Hoshangabad 15 each; Khargone 13; Cherrapunji & Buldana 11 each; Wardha 10; Indore & Brahmapuri 9 each; Chandrapur 8; Akola, Nagpur & Adilabad 7 each; Silchar, Betul, Amroati & Washim 6 each; Lengpui 5 and Halflong & Rajgarh 4 each.
